Intership Artworks & Designs

Down below are artowrks and designs that I made during my internships. Each section will give the title or concept name, date, company I interned for, and a short synopsys of relevant info and background info about the works.

Try The World: Graphic Design Intern. Try The World is an international snack monthly subscription box company.

Email Creatives, 2024, Try The World

I was tasked to create email creatives for brand outreaches for various snacks from countries all over the world. Strategically, the color pallets I used for each creative reuses the same pallet from the product packages to maintain the design and color language throughout the whole design.

Composite Concepts, 2024, Try The World

For these designs, I created product and location composites to highlight the beauty of the product's country of origin and product itself to attach the location to a brand. I was tasked to create concepts based upon a new possible strategy to do for social media advertising and promotion.

Snack Madness, 2024, Try The World

During March Madness, we created our own basketball tournament themed competition that invloved various internation snacks to crown the best snack. Part of the interaction that the followers had with the tournament was voting on the winning snack per individual face-off for each bracket. I created two Instagram stories that were used for the poll voting feature for the competition.

March Posts, 2024, Try The World

For the month of March, I was tasked with posts to make for various social media platforms. I had to stick to a general theme and had to create special observable holiday posts as well.

May Posts, 2024, Try The World

For the month of May, I was tasked with posts and concepts to make for various social media platforms. I had to stick to a general theme and had to create special observable holiday posts as well.

Snack Beast Posts, 2024, Try The World
