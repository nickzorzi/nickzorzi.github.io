Oil Rig Enviroment, 2024, Art 102

Creating 3D models in Blender to be arranged and designed into a oil rig game enviroment in Unity. All images and recordings of the oil rig enviroment are taken through the Unity gameplay camera. This shows the concept game enviroment as a functioning environment for a possible real video game development use.
