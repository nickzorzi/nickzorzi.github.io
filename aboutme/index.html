Artist Biography

Bay Area based Digital Media Artist, Nicolas Zorzi, has been creating and designing digital artworks for many years. With a focus on interactive art, he blends videos and digital artworks with code to create an engaging and captivating audience experience.

He focuses on world building and the narrative of his artworks in addition to the medium to allow for a deeper complexity. This is the result of his interest in embracing his own cultural and national background. Since his birth, Nicolas has been well traveled due to being a child of immigrant parents. This allowed for him to see and experience various cultures and environments first hand, which would influence the way he creates and values art.
