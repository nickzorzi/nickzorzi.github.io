Sound Bounce (Project 4)

Click '<<' & '>>' buttons or 'A' & 'D' keys to activate flipper.

Click 'GO' button or 'Spacebar' key to launch the ball.

Use keys '1,2,3' to navigate the scenes or use the key 'H' to open up the help scene.

Point System: Walls = 1 point, Big Circle = 5 points, & Small Circle = 10 points.
