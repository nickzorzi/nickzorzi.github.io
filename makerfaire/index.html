Micro Perform, 2024, Art 106

Using the Raspberry Pi Pico board, I created an interactive and engaging sound maker. The project is housed in a found object, cardboard box, where buttons and inputs are installed on the top side, the speaker is installed on top, combined with a 3d printed amplifier cone, photocells are installed on the back, amplifier knob extends out on the right. It utilizes the Arduino IDE software to play and modulate (tempo, octave, note length, and track playing) 4 total midi tracks from both Paganini and Bach through the analog and digital inputs, while lighting 2 LEDs for light level, and 1 for tempo and status.
