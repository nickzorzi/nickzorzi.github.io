Art 75

Films from the Art 75 Class

Down below are 3 films I created for the Intro to Video Editing class (Art 75, Professor David Bayus)

Shrink Film, 2022

For the Shrink Film, I decided to create a short story about, how with a remote controller, I can shrink my dog and take him around. Using a greenscreen and motion tracking on After Effects, I was successfully able to make my dog appear as if he was the size of my hand.

10pm, 2022

The purpose of this project is to properly display time in a cinematic story. In this short film, I tell the story of a school boy who comes home from school and does homework until 10pm hits and the school boy transcends into a new world where time starts to speed up.

The Screwdriver, 2022

For my final project of the class, I created a comedy horror short film about a screwdriver that falls down from space and is picked up by a group of teenagers who are hunted down for that very screwdriver. The short film inhibits concepts that were taught and used throughout the semester, such as: green screening, motion tracking, color grading, sound design, and etc.
